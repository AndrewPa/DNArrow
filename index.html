Detailed Instructions

Enter the active/inactive region coordinates into their respective input forms. These can be typed in manually one at a time, or many at once, with each region separated by a single space.

These can also be added by copying (sorted) columns from an excel file and pasting the results into their respective input boxes.

In either case, the format should be as follows:

[CoordinateA1];[CoordinateA2] [CoordinateB1];[CoordinateB2]

e.g.: 100;200 200;300 300;400 etc.

Please ensure that left and right bounds are separated by a semicolon (;) and that only spaces separate each region.

When ready to submit, press "enter" or click on the appropriate "add" button.

You may delete any coordinate from either of the two input boxes by selecting them and pressing the "delete" key on your keyboard.
